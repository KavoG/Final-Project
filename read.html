HOW TO READ A BOOK

Type of reading: Analytical Reading

Before reading...
Have a goal (to learn/gain profit)
Title
Table of Contents
Conclusion of Chapters or Book
While Reading...
Find out EXACTLY what type of book it is
Write down Questions you have about the book
Note the most used and technical words
Write the most important pieces of information (usually the problem solving answers)
After Reading...
Answer: What was the book about?
What is being said in detail and how?
Is the book true (In whole or in part)?
What of it (meaning)?
If you feel compelled enough, write a theme about the topic.

Type of reading: Syntopical Reading

Before reading...
Know EXACTLY what you wish to be enlightened on
Have all the books at your disposal
Assess all the books just as in an Analytical Read
While Reading...
Preferrably, read similar sections right after another
Build a set of terms with the authors
Have questions, but in neutral propositions; questions all books will answer
Write the most important pieces of information (usually the problem solving answers)
After Reading...
Reflect on your enlightenment
Answer: Does progress occur in history
You must do a writing on the topic
Change something in your life in order to pay respects
